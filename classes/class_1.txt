Dinner Class

Attributes of Dinner Class:
1. protein (string)
2. starch (string)
3. vegetable (string)
4. price (float)
5. in_stock (boolean)

Methods of Dinner Class:
1. update_stock (Changes the in_stock attribute to true or false)
2. discount (Reduces the price attribute to apply a discount of $1.00)
3. make_vegetarian (Removes the protein and adds another vegetable)
4. change_starch (Changes the starch attribute to another starch)
